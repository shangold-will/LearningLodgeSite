Tutoring Resources

If your student does not have a set curriculum, here are some free, online resources that we recommend:

  • ABCmouse (temporarily free)
    • (Grades K-2) Reading, Math, and Science
  • Khan Academy Recommended
    • Math (Grades K-12), English Grammar, and Reading (Grades 2-8)
  • EDSITEment
    • History and Social Studies (Grades K-12)
    • Literature and Language Arts (Grades K-12)
  • Conjugemos
    • Foreign Language (Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Korean, and Latin) (Grades 6-8)
  • Scholastic
    • All Subjects: lesson plans and hands-on activities (Grades K-9)
  • Math Pyramid
    • Math: worksheets (Grades K-3)
  • National Geographic Kids Recommended
    • Explore nature through videos and quizzes
  • PBS Kids Games Recommended
    • Tons of fun games for math, vocabulary, spelling, reading, science, and social studies.
  • Sight Words
    • Learning to Read: video lessons, lesson plans, and games (Grades K-2)
  • Science Kids
    • Science: fact pages, quizzes, and hands-on activities (Grades K-5)
  • International Children's Digital Library
    • Reading (Grades K-9)
    • Foreign Language (Grades 6-8)
  • Virtual Field Trips Recommended
    • Explore the San Diego zoo, Yellowstone National Park, Mars, and more through virtual field trips!
  • Resource List with More Resources
  • Comprehensive Resource Page

If you know of any additional online resources that you would like to add to this list, please let us know at ElementaryOnlineTutoring@gmail.com or MiddleSchoolOnlineTutoring@gmail.com to submit new resources.
